About Me:

Though I grew up in the beautiful Blue Ridge Mountains in Blacksburg, Virginia, I’m happy to call Denver my home since earning my undergraduate music degree here.

I started exploring programming in my spare time a few years ago and have since decided to pursue it as a career. In fall 2018, I graduated from Turing School of Software and Design, and have since then been on the job hunt, while also continuing to explore new technologies and practice my skills.

I’ve played the guitar since I was about 12 years old, and music will always be a big part of my life. As and experienced sideman, session musician, teacher, singer/songwriter, and bandleader, I’ve had the privilege to travel to places around the country and the world I would other wise not have, and for this I am eternally grateful. Currently, I front an original rock band called Crimson Crows, and do occasional solo- or duo gigs with my dad, who plays professionally in the mountains.

Otherwise, I enjoy hiking, snowboarding, mountain biking, live music, a good beer, and generally exploring new places and meeting diverse and interesting people.
