About Me:

Though I grew up in the beautiful Blue Ridge Mountains in Blacksburg, Virginia, I’m happy to call Denver my home since earning my undergraduate music degree here.

I started exploring programming in my spare time a few years ago and have since decided to pursue it as a career. In fall 2018, I graduated from Turing School of Software and Design, and have since then continued to explore new technologies and develop my skills. I love the puzzle solving aspect of coding, and the way it feels like building little digital machines that get jobs done. My strongest stack is React/Redux with Node and Postgres.

Making music has been a major part of my life since I picked up the guitar at age 12 and subsequently fell in love with composition and songwriting. My musical influences range from jazz and American roots music to punk-rock and rock-steady ska. As and experienced sideman, session musician, teacher, singer/songwriter, and bandleader, I’ve had the privilege to travel to places around the country and the world I would other wise not have, and for this I am eternally grateful. Currently, I front an original rock band called Crimson Crows, and do occasional solo performances, or duo gigs with my dad, who plays professionally in the Colorado high country.

When I'm not coding or playing an instrument, I enjoy hiking, snowboarding, mountain biking, live music, a good beer, and generally exploring new places and meeting diverse and interesting people.
